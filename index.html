a little about me

calm, collected, and a little bit extra

I care about doing things properly. Quality over quantity, always put together, never an afterthought. Whether I'm hosting twelve people with zero notice, helping a friend find the right words for a hard conversation, or making sure ten closings run without a single hiccup, I show up the same way: composed, generous, and ready to make it happen.

By day, I'm the Operations Manager at Beach State Realty, keeping a team of 10+ Realtors running smoothly. Transaction coordination, marketing, social media, and all the behind-the-scenes details that make real estate feel effortless. I'm a licensed Realtor myself, too. I closed over $1,000,000 in my first year, so I know exactly how a transaction works from start to finish, and I'm good at explaining it in plain English.
